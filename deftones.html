Brief History of Deftones

Deftones is a rock band from Sacramento, California. They formed in 1988 and have released 12 studio albums. They are active and their current lineup is:

  • Chino Moreno
  • Stephen Carpenter
  • Abe Cunningham
  • Frank Delgado
